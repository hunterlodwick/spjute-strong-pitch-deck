Reducing Your 14.1% Payment Failure Rate

Last year, $49,818 in payments failed. You recovered $36,847 — but $12,971 was permanently lost. A 14.1% failure rate means 1 in 7 payment attempts is failing. Here's how we help drive that rate down:

🚫

Block Prepaid Cards

Prepaid and gift cards are the #1 source of failed recurring payments. We'll help you configure your processor to decline prepaid cards as a payment method — only real debit/credit cards accepted.

🔄

Auto Follow-Ups on Missed Payments

When a payment fails, the system automatically sends SMS + email reminders to the client with a secure payment link. No manual chasing — Haley doesn't have to track down every failed charge.

💳

Card Updater Service

When clients' cards expire or get reissued, the processor automatically pulls updated card details from the issuing bank — preventing failed charges before they happen.

Why Dual Pricing — Not Surcharging

Dual Pricing ✓

  • ✓ Two prices displayed upfront — card price and cash/ACH price
  • ✓ Legal in all 50 states — no restrictions
  • ✓ Feels like a discount, not a penalty
  • ✓ No negative customer perception
  • ✓ Encourages ACH/Zelle adoption → fewer chargebacks

Surcharging ✕

  • ✕ Adds a fee on top of the listed price at checkout
  • ✕ Illegal in several states (CT, MA, PR, etc.)
  • ✕ Feels like a punishment for paying with a card
  • ✕ Increases cart abandonment and client frustration
  • ✕ Subject to Visa/Mastercard surcharge caps and rules
With dual pricing, there's no extra fee — just two clearly posted prices. Your clients choose. The experience is clean, transparent, and never feels like a penalty. It's built into the system seamlessly from day one.
